Category

Cake

Baked sweet dessert that typically consists of flour, sugar, eggs, and butter or oil. Cakes come in diverse forms, from simple single-layer cakes to elaborate multi-tiered creations, and are often adorned with frosting, icing, or decorations for added visual appeal.

Ice-cream

Frozen dessert made from a mixture of cream, milk, sugar, and flavorings. It is typically churned to incorporate air and create a smooth and creamy texture. Ice cream comes in various flavors, ranging from classic vanilla and chocolate to more unique combinations. 

Others

Wide array of sweet treats beyond traditional cakes and ice cream. This diverse category may include puddings, tarts, pies, pastries, cookies, custards, and more. These desserts offer a delightful variety of flavors, textures, and culinary creativity to satisfy different preferences and cravings.
